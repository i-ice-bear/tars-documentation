# Target audience

The “Tars Desktop.“ targets students of all age groups, particularly those students and educational institutions who can’t able to afford an expensive laptop or computer. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, making it suitable for primary, middle, and high school students. It caters to both those with basic computer literacy and those looking to enhance their digital skills. Like Office workloads, Aggressive programming, Medicore programming, and other usual tasks. 
The project also aims to benefit educators, including teachers and mentors. The platform empowers teachers to integrate technology into their teaching methodologies, create interactive learning materials, and encourage active participation among students.
Overall, the “Tars. Desktop” project seeks to create a dynamic and inclusive learning environment, inspiring curiosity, creativity, and continuous learning among students and educators alike.
